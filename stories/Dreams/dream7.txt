Title: Nightmare deluxe - Being buried alive in an avalanche while sleeping in an actual tent in an avalanche area

I usually have the most bizarre dreams and nightmares but this took the first prize in scaryness. I'm currently on a hiking trip to Norway and living in a tent. They have a serious avalache risk warning in my area and while I sleep in a safe area (I think) there are still huge snowy mountains nearby and my brain love to torture me as usual.

The dream was simple, my tent was buried in a huge avalanche. I woke up (didn't know it was a dream at that point), was pitch black outside, had my earplugs in (that I forgotten) so the outside sound was cut off too for extra immersive effect. I jumped up, started to calculate how long I had left to live and slowly opened the tent door to see if it was a wall of snow outside. Luckily there was not and I put my head out for a long time and just took deep breaths of the fresh cold air.

I was wearing my watch and it displayed a heart rate of 150. Took two hours to calm down and go back to sleep. But half sleeping and every car that later drew by, I woke up because they kind of sounded like avalanches coming in at 100km/h 😅 I wish you all safe sleep without this crap!
