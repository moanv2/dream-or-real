Title: Seance gone wrong

Me and a woman I don't seem to remember wanted to do a seance, we wanted to summon a ghost to give us some sort of wisdom. During the seance we were supposed to remain eye contact with the spirit at all times. It went wrong immediately: our pupils would become dilated and our bodies started to shake violently out of control. To conclude the seance we had to find our doppelgangers in order to sacrifice them to the spirit world and save ourselves.

I feel like this dream is a very important warning for me.
