Title: Crazy dream last night. Hope it gives you some entertainment

It all started with me at work. I was 5 hours in the shift, had about 20 shopping carts lined up when I hear this really loud crash. Turns out there was a car accident and someone rammed a parked car. The woman in the parked car lost her face (think Amish doll). Turns out the woman who hit her was drunk and high on meth. She was really erratic and the cops tasers were ineffective so they had to blast her with a flamethrower to subdue her. After all that went down the store manager shut down the store for the day so I went to my second job as a food critic for the Gambino crime family. They would have me go to their fake restaurants and leave positive reviews so it looked legit.
