Title: I had a dream that my VPN app got weirdly patriotic

So in real life my phone's VPN app has a button called "Fastest country" that picks the fastest connection.

Last night I had a dream that my internet was broken and my browser would not load anything, so I opened the app to fix it. The normal button was gone and instead there was a huge new one that said "GREATEST COUNTRY 🇺🇲 🇺🇲 🇺🇲" in all caps.

I tapped it and instantly everything worked. I opened my browser and pages started loading instantly.
