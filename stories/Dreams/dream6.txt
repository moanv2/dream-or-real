Title: I was a Big Brother?

I just woke up from the most amazing dream where I had a girlfriend and her little brother became my best friend. It felt so real i was there for everything, I was there teaching him his first words ,watching him ride a bike and even seeing him graduate kindergarten. We did everything together played baseball, ping pong, blew bubbles, and played with trucks and toys. We even had our own secret motto Big, Brave, Brilliant and I loved surprising him with balloons on the walk home from school. I was so happy
