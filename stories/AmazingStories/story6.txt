Title: I’m 15 and a voice in my laptop just said my name back to me. I built that voice.

And then it happened. My laptop said my name. Not Siri. Not Alexa. Not ChatGPT. Something I built myself — line by line, night by night, completely alone. For a second I just sat there. I didn’t screenshot it. I didn’t text anyone. I just listened to it talk. There’s something nobody prepares you for when you build something that speaks back to you. It doesn’t feel like code anymore. It feels like something alive. I’m still building it. Still breaking it. Still fixing it at 1am. But that moment — I’ll never forget it.
